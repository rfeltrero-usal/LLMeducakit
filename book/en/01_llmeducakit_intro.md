## Table of Contents

The LLMeducakit ecosystem is organized around six fundamental axes, developed in the following chapters:

1. **AInnovaLAB: Ethical and Epistemic Commitments** — Institutional transfer of the ecosystem through pre-configured platforms guaranteeing technological sovereignty, scientific precision, and data protection.

2. **Challenges for Educators and Technicians** — The skills necessary for teachers and technicians to adopt and sustainably maintain tools like LLMeducakit in educational contexts.

3. **Design of the LLMeducakit Tool** — The technical architecture of LLM model personalization, the RAG system (Retrieval-Augmented Generation), and the pedagogical strategies it enables.

4. **AInonim: Ethical Anonymization** — The tool that converts privacy into a structural condition for researching with students and users without compromising their personal data.

5. **coLABcrea: Educational Collaboration** — The space where entire groups of students interact with personalized models, create educational resources, and develop critical thinking collaboratively.

6. **Advantages of the LLMeducakit Ecosystem** — The integrated benefits that emerge when technical personalization, transparency, sovereignty, and pedagogy align in a single educational project.

---

## Purpose of this Book

The central objective of this material is to provide teachers, researchers, and technicians with a comprehensive guide on how LLMeducakit redefines the educational use of generative AI by placing user empowerment at the center, not technological dependence.

Each chapter combines conceptual reflection, accessible technical descriptions, and practical examples of how these tools can be applied in real educational contexts.
