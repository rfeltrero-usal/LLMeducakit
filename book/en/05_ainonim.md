# 5. AInonim: Ethical Anonymization in the Ecosystem

*(English translation pending - Spanish version available)*

This chapter presents AInonim as a fundamental component of LLMeducakit — the ethical anonymization tool that enables research with real interactions between students and AI without exposing personal identities or compromising privacy.

Key concepts:
- Privacy as structural condition, not afterthought
- Epistemic traceability without personal identification
- Research-ready data while protecting participants
- Integration with RAG, adaptive tutoring, and bias detection
- Technological sovereignty and local data processing
- AInonim's role in empowering educators ethically
