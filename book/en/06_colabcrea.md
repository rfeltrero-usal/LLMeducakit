# 6. coLABcrea: Collaborative Tool for Shared Learning

*(English translation pending - Spanish version available)*

This chapter describes coLABcrea as the collaborative interface where entire groups of students interact simultaneously with personalized AI models, enabling collective construction of educational resources and critical thinking development.

Covered topics:
- Group interaction with pedagogically situated AI
- The role of the AI: guide, content provider, tutor, critical counterpoint, ethical auditor
- Collaborative creation of educational resources
- Integration with RAG for knowledge-anchored group work
- Critical thinking and debate scaffolding
- Adaptive group feedback and ethical auditing
- Teacher empowerment in pedagogical design
- Building educational innovation communities
