## A Platform to Empower Educators and Researchers with Generative Artificial Intelligence

This book documents the principles, tools, and strategies of the **LLMeducakit** ecosystem: an open-source solution designed for teachers and researchers from all disciplines to personalize large language models (LLMs) and create innovative educational experiences while maintaining full control over their infrastructure, data, and processes.

Through the following chapters, we explore how LLMeducakit translates principles of technological sovereignty, ethics, and algorithmic transparency into practical tools for higher education and research.
